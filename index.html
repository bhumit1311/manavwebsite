About Me

Crafting Stories Frame by Frame

I'm Manav — a professional video editor and visual storyteller based in India with over 2 years of experience shaping raw footage into compelling cinematic narratives. My journey began with a deep passion for films and evolved into a career working across commercials, weddings, YouTube channels, and brand films.

I believe every frame is an opportunity to evoke emotion. Whether I'm crafting a 30-second Instagram reel or a 20-minute wedding documentary, my philosophy remains constant: tell the truest, most beautiful version of every story.
